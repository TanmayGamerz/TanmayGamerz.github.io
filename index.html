Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma is an Indian cricketer who currently plays and captains the India national cricket team. He is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities, and leadership skills. Sharma is a right-handed batsman and has held several batting records, including most double centuries in ODI cricket and most centuries in the Cricket World Cup. He has played for Mumbai Indians in IPL and domestic cricket, winning five titles under his leadership. 

Sharma is the most successful captain in IPL history, sharing the record with MS Dhoni.
He has also played in every edition of the ICC T20 World Cup, winning the ICC Men's ODI Cricketer of the Year award in 2019. Sharma has received two national honours, the Arjuna Award in 2015 and the prestigious Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award in 2020. Under his captaincy, India won the 2018 Asia Cup and the 2023 Asia Cup, the seventh and eighth time the country won the title. Sharma is also an active supporter of animal welfare campaigns, serving as the official Rhino Ambassador for WWF-India and a member of PETA.


Elon Musk

Elon Musk,the founder, chairman, CEO, and chief technology officer of SpaceX, Tesla, X Corp., Boring Company, xAI, Neuralink, OpenAI, and president of the Musk Foundation, is the wealthiest person in the world with an estimated net worth of 
      $232 billion      
as of December 2023. Born in Pretoria, he attended Queen's University and later transferred to the University of Pennsylvania. Musk co-founded Zip2, X.com, PayPal, SolarCity, OpenAI, Neuralink, Boring Company, and Twitter. In 2022, he acquired Twitter for       $44 billion       and merged it into X Corp., rebranding the service as X. In March 2023, he founded xAI. Musk has faced criticism for making unscientific statements, including COVID-19 misinformation, transphobia, and antisemitic conspiracy theories. In 2018, the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission sued him for falsely tweeting that he had secured funding for a private takeover of Tesla.
